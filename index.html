The Product — Lumiclean

Lumiclean is a genetically modified organism developed to both detect and degrade pollutants in water.

How detection works

Equipped with the lux operon, Lumiclean produces visible bioluminescence when it encounters target pollutants — a simple, immediate indicator of contamination.

How degradation works

Engineered metabolic pathways break down harmful compounds into harmless byproducts, aiding natural recovery of water bodies.

Safety & deployment

Deployment is performed under regulatory and safety protocols by authorized teams. Field trials and monitoring ensure environmental stewardship.
